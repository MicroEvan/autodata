Overview

Auto Data is a modern digital diagnostic service founded in 2025. We specialize in advanced vehicle data analysis, fault detection, automotive system diagnostics, and mechanical repair. By combining cutting-edge technology with hands-on garage expertise, we deliver fast, accurate, and reliable solutions.

To provide precise, data-driven vehicle diagnostics that help customers and workshops make smarter repair decisions, reduce downtime, and improve vehicle performance.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name in the automotive repair industry by bridging technology and mechanical expertise.
